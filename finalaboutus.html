About Us

Astro-Adventures is a forum where people can post about their various experiences in the cosmos. Founded by two ex-astronauts, Richard Percival, and Hyunji Kim.

This website was founded after one of Hyunji's many spelunking trips into the craters of Saturn's moon, Titan. Hyunji saw an amazing rock formation and wanted to let the whole world experience it! However, there was no cosmos wide adventure website available to her. Hyunji sought out her good friend Richard Percival, and with his assistance they created this website for everyone to share their adventures within the cosmos!
