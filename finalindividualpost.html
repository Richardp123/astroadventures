Trip to Italy

-- The beginning of Italian travel --

I have finally started my Italian travel posting. In fact, Italy, Spain, and Turkey among the countries that have traveled so far have been hesitant to start because there are many stories to talk about, but I should start writing now. I want to start my first trip to Italy today.First of all, let me give you the overall schedule. If you are traveling only in Italy for 1-2 weeks, you should try to follow my schedule.

It seems like many people have traveled to Milan out of Rome, because Milan has more flights and more routes to France or Switzerland. I decided to go out to Italy with this trip because it is a schedule to travel to Italy. There is nothing special about it, no matter whether you go first in Milan or Florence first.

-- Great Architecture Colosseum --

I will introduce the whole schedule in short before leaving a real travel review. First of all, I arrived in the city of Rome where the civilization of the year 2000 lives and the past and present coexist. The first place I went was the Colosseum, the symbol of Rome!

-- Companion to travel to Italy Gelato --

After that, you have to visit Jerusalem and visit the three greatest gelato shops in the city. Even if it is not Rome, you can actually taste fine gelato in any of the gelato shops in the neighborhood. The personally memorable Gelato shop was a shabby gelato shop in front of Santa Maria Cathedral in Florence. It is the best store in the caustic sardine, so I always went there!

-- The Temple of the Gods Pantheon --

The atmosphere of the Pantheon differs day and night so much that it has to come once a day during the day. Rome is a beautiful city at night, and it is not wrong at all. If you visit the Pantheon during the day, you will be proud of the elegance of the temple, but if you come back at night, you will feel magnificence.
